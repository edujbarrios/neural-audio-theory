Welcome to **Neural Audio Theory** — an open educational resource exploring the engineering foundations of AI music generation.

{/* truncate */}

## What This Project Covers

This documentation site breaks down how modern text-to-music and audio generation systems work, including:

- **Signal Processing Fundamentals** — FFT, spectrograms, and audio representations
- **Neural Architectures** — Transformers and diffusion models adapted for audio
- **Latent Space Geometry** — How models organize musical concepts mathematically
- **Prompt Engineering** — Practical conditioning strategies based on ML behavior

## Who Is This For?

Whether you're:

- A **music producer** wanting to understand AI tools better
- A **developer** curious about audio ML systems
- A **researcher** looking for accessible explanations
- A **creator** trying to get more consistent AI music outputs

You'll find content ranging from beginner-friendly overviews to engineering-depth technical explanations.
